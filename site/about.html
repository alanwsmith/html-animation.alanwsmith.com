This piece consists of eight frames of ascii art based on "The Horse in Motion" by Eadweard Muybridge

Animation between frames is done with `<meta http-equiv="refresh">` tags. Chrome allows times less than a second (e.g. '0.3') which would provide a smoother animation, but Safari and Firefox require whole seconds. So, this version is at 1fps

If you're in Chrome, you can check out the smoother version here
